The Negative Impacts of a Digital Life

02


User:
The negative impact of a digital life that I chose to focus on is
how social media and the rise of photoshopped images distorts many
digital user’s perception of reality. Many young people often posts
perfect pictures on platforms such as Facebook, Twitter,
Youtube, and in this example, Instagram. The purpose of this is
usually just to share or advertise experiences and products on social
media. However, with this, also comes with the formation of a false
sense of reality in which users sometimes attempt to achieve —
affecting their mental health.

Interface:
There are many interface features on Instagram that
encourages sharing and posting through its Instagram feed, stories,
TV and direct messaging. For example, the feed and discover page
are both designed as an endless scroll — allowing users to use
the app for hours on end. Instagram stories encourages users to
constantly keep checking the app for updates on their following’s
stories as they only appear for 24 hours and is regularly updated.

Address:
Wifi is needed to access Instagram. Hence, usually
people would be using their home wifi or their mobile data
via 4G or LTE.

City:
Currently living in Shanghai, NYU Shanghai students
would all be relying on some sort of Chinese internet
provider, perhaps China Telecom. Since we are in China,
VPN is required to access Instagram.

Cloud:
All Instagram users and profile information would
all be stored in the Instagram server. This huge server
allows the organisation and storage of user data and
interaction.

Earth:
rom anywhere around the world, anyone could post
whatever content they desire on Instagram. In this image,
a person is photoshopping an image to get it ready for
posting on Instagram in order for the image to look
“better”. Whilst that may be the case, doing this simply
creates a false image of perfection that may not be attainable
to its audience. Once again, pressures and possibly
mental health issues may arise from this act — a negative
impact of a digital life.
